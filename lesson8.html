Other prepositions are more abstract and are harder to find a picture for, so i will include them as they are:

at - usually indicates a location or time

at 2 o'clock
at school
at work

with - con

tea with sugar
i will play with you

without - sin

coffee without sugar
do you like your tea with or without sugar?

for - por, para

do it for me
will you buy it for me?

about - sobre

did you talk about me?
let's talk about it
