"When" and "If" with will

Great job on that speaking practice! Let's take a litle break and look into some other important aspects. In this section we will talk about the usage of "when" and "if" with will. The main thing is "don't do it". Simple is that. Words "when" and "if" simply don't go together.

When you see her, say hi - Cuando la veas, dile hola
if i see you, i will go home - Si te veo, me iré a casa

Imperative

In imperative form we basically tell a person what to do

Go! - ¡Ve!
Come! - ¡Ven!
Don't play! - ¡No juegues!

When you ask someone to do something with you we use the word "let's" which is short for "let us". Let's take a look at some examples

Let's go! - ¡Vamos!
Let's jump! - ¡Saltemos!
Let's play! - ¡Juguemos!
Let's read - ¡Leamos!
