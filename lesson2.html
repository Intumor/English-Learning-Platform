Most words that end with "sion" and "tion" in English end with "sión" and "ción" in Spanish

sion - sión
tion - ción

For Example:

Conclusion - Conclusión
Discussion - Discusión
Decision - Decisión
Mission - Misión
Expansion - Expansión

Information - Información
Presentation - Presentación
Interaction - Interacción
Communication - Comunicación
Organization - Organización

Pronouns

Just like in Spanish, in English we have pronouns that are used as the subjects of sentences

I - Yo
You - Tú
We - Nosotros
They - Ellos
He - Él
She - Ella

And there are also pronouns that are used as the objects of verbs or prepositions

I - me
You - you
We - us
They - them
He - him
She - her

Yo - Me
Tú - Te | Usted - Le | Vosotros - Os | Ustedes - Les
Nosotros - Nos | Nosotras - Nos
Ellos - Los | Ellas - Las
Él - Lo
Ella - La

For Example:

I see him - Lo veo
He sees me - Él me ve
They see us - Ellos nos ven

So, the words in the first column are linked to "who" and words in the second column are linked to "whom"
